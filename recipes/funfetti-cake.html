Funfetti Cake: A Sweet Treat for All!

Description

Looking for an appetizing and rewarding dessert? Or maybe an easy cake to help unwind after a long day? look no further becasue you found something simple, quick, and flavorful.

We will take you through the cake batter and frosting becasue what's a cake without its topping!

Ingredients

Cake:

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 4 large egg whites
  • 2 tablespoons clear imitation vanilla
  • 6 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 1/2 cups cake flour
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk of your choice
  • 2/3 cup sprinkles

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Grease a 8" x 11" pan.
  3. Mix together the butter and sugar until the mixture is fluffy.
  4. Add the egg whites, one at a time, mixing well after each one.
  5. Mix in the vanilla and oil.
  6. Add and mix the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  7. Finish off the batter by folding in the milk and sprinkles until smooth.
  8. Add the batter to the pan and place it in the oven for 35 minutes.
    9. Note: Baking time may differ, double check the doneness by poking a fork in the middle until no cake batter comes up with it.
  9. Let the cake cool completely before frosting.

Ingredients

Frosting

  • 1 cup vegetable shortening, or vegan butter
  • 2 – 4 tablespoons milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon coarse salt
  • 4 – 5 cups powdered sugar

Depending on which flavor of frosting you are wanting, we need to tweak the recipe.

To make funfetti frosting, gently mix in some rainbow sprinkles at the end.

To make chocolate frosting, add 1/4 cup of cocoa powder with the wet ingredients.

To make strawberry frosting, add 1/4 cup strawberry puree or strawberry jam.

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, beat the shortening, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth and fluffy.
  2. Add salt and powdered sugar.
